What is the Pomley Marketplace?

July 2, 2022

The Pomley marketplace brings the best of eCommerce and blockchain technology, pivoting web-two shopping into a web-three experience. Unlike our core contenders, the Pomley marketplace continues to accomplish anonymous shopping of millions of goods while achieving the highest level of protection and sustainability in web-three. As you may have detected, our web-two competitors neglect essential web-three processes that facilitate user safety and experience. Pomley will reinvent online shopping through guerrilla marketing, an all-in approach to web-three, and mass-user involvement.

Shopping / Selling on Pomley

Shopping on the Pomley marketplace allows users to browse products by the leading brands in the market. The team has designed features to foster anonymity, security, and user experience in the web-three environment. The following are samples of components you can explore on the Pomley marketplace:

  • Pomley Web:
    Access the Pomley marketplace on your personal computer via your favorite browsers. Create a profile connected to a web-three wallet or use a traditional email and password.

  • Pomley App:
    Download the Pomley marketplace on your mobile device for fast access. Generate a user profile by connecting a web-three wallet or using the conventional email and password option.
